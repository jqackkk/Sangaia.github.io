Quick Facts

Many people associate propaganda with censorship or brainwashing, but the Ministry of Propaganda does the exact opposite. While it does permit what can be shown to the public on broadcasting systems such as electronic mail or television, it doesn't do so from a political standpoint. It also administers what churches are allowed to be created and monitors their spending should their belief system support wealth-redistribution to the community.

The Ministry of the Treasury receives random, unannounced check-ins from members of the Body of Electives. This prevents fraud and ensures that money is going to where it belongs.

The Ministry of Work existed before the actual Sangaian Government was even thought of. It has evolved over time as Sangaia went through its revolutions.
