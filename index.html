Better for Everyone. Including You.

Sangaia is meant to fix what its predecessor, the United States, could not. Offering cheaper land, better home settings, more freedoms, a better foundation for democracy and republicanism, and a balanced monetary system, all united within provincial territories which are easily understood and navigable.

Reinvented Politics. Constitutionally Ruled.

Sangaia has invented a new form of government: The Aeretocracy. Unlike other nations with differing political ideas, we don't force our oppositions into labor camps, kill others who speak against us, or have unfair, inhumane rules; we don't censor other people and occupy hundreds of native tribes who don't even know we exist; and we don't force our children into labor, or disrespect the privacy of our citizens.

Sangaia offers almost identical rules and structures as the outdated, yet somehow loved, below-average American Democracy. The difference? It's structured so that no one–not even the Aeretocrat–can break it.
